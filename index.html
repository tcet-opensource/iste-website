About Us

WHO ARE WE ? Learn More About Us!

The Indian Society for Technical Education is a national professional, non-profit society registered under the Indian Societies Registration Act of 1860. TCET-ISTE is a professional body of the ES&H department of TCET. ISTE is responsible for technical events in the college with a focus on the personality development of the students.


As members of this technical education club, we have the opportunity to create a community that fosters innovation and learning. Together, we can explore new ideas, push boundaries, and make a difference in the world of technology. Through our club, we can connect with like-minded individuals who share our passion for technology and collaborate to create something great. As members of this club, we have the opportunity to make a positive impact on our community and the world at large through our innovative ideas and projects. Let's use this platform to network, build relationships, and create opportunities for ourselves and others.


OUR MISSION

TCET-ISTE's mission is to help learners thrive in a connected world by fostering a passionate skilled learning culture, leveraging experience and skills, and continuously enhancing learning and teaching, resulting in a symbiotic environment for all departments. The ISTE-TCET Student's chapter is pleased to announce that it has attracted more than 500 members from various departments every year.

OUR VISION

TCET-ISTE's vision is to provide high-quality training programmes for the first year engineering students in our institute so that they can keep their knowledge and skills updated in today's technical world. It also focuses on providing students with feedback and instruction in order to help them improve their learning skills and personalities.
